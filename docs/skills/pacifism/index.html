Pacifism Mastery

Greatly reduces incoming damage from hostile players (PvP).

  • Hostile Players' Damage -X%

Skill bonuses

Hostile Players' Damage -X%

  • Functionally equivalent to Damage Mitigation +X%, but applies only in a player vs. player scenario.
  • To convert it to Damage Mitigation +X%, invert the sign of X.
    • Example: If the tooltip shows Hostile Players' Damage -18.0%, the equivalent is Damage Mitigation +18.0%.
  • Stacks additively with other sources of Damage Mitigation +X%.
