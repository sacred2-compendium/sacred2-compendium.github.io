Modification details

Concealment

Increases evade chance inside the cloud.

Recondition

Significantly reduces cooldown.

Elongate

Dust Devil lasts considerably longer.

Spell Shield

Light protection from hostile spells.

Sanding

Slightly damages opponents inside the Dust Devil.

Spread

Increases circumference of Dust Devil significantly.
