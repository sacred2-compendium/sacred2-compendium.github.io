BeeEffGee (Weapon)

The BeeEffGee is a two-handed energy weapon (SUBFAM_PRI_ENERGY_WEAPON).

It is a legendary item (item tier 15).

The governing attribute is Dexterity.

Without modifying the game files, the only way to obtain this weapon is to summon it through BeeEffGee (Buff). When obtained this way:

  • The item's level will vary from 2 to 200.
  • It will receive additional bonuses from BeeEffGee (Buff), like Chance to pierce +X% or extra physical damage.
