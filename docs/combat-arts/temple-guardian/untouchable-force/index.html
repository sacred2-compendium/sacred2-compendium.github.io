Modification details

Field Damage

Forcefield inflicts increased magic damage.

Mind Control

Increases chance to stun nearby opponents.

Paralyze

Decreases movement speed of nearby opponents.

Calm

Reduces attack speed of nearby opponents.

Crumble

Some projectiles are destroyed upon impact without causing damage.
