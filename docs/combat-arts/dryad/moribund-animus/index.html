Moribund Animus (Buff)

Summons a hireling whose type is based on the shrunken head the Dryad currently has equipped (with no shrunken head equipped, a ghost will be conjured); also increases the chance that opponents will drop shrunken heads. Deactivating Moribund Animus will release the summoned hireling.

  • Regeneration Time of all Combat Arts +X%
  • Chance for shrunken head X%
  • Hireling Level X
