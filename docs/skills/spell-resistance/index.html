Spell Resistance Mastery

Increases the spell resistance as well as the chance to evade critical hits. Reduces the duration of detrimental effects.

  • Spell Resistance +X%
  • Evade Critical Hits -X%
  • Reduction of Detrimental Effects +X%

Skill bonuses

Spell Resistance +X%

  • This bonus is non-functional. It does not increase the character's Spell Resistance stat, despite what the tooltip suggests.
  • Instead, the skill grants the bonus Opponent's chance to overcome spell resistance -X%.

Opponent's chance to overcome spell resistance -X%

  • Not shown in the skill tooltip, but confirmed via testing. Name is unofficial, but reflects observed behavior.
  • Has the same progression as the Evade Critical Hits -X% bonus.
  • Reduces the opponent's chance to succeed at the spell intensity vs spell resistance check by the given percentage. This is a relative reduction.
    • Example: If the base chance to succeed is 80%, and the bonus is -50%, then the chance is reduced to 40%.
  • This bonus is unique to the skill Spell Resistance.

Evade Critical Hits -X%

  • This bonus is non-functional. It has no effect on the chance to receive critical hits.
  • The only known way to reduce the chance of receiving critical hits is by mastering the skill Combat Reflexes, which grants a similar bonus that does work, even against spells.

Reduction of Detrimental Effects +X%

  • Requires skill mastery (unlocked at skill level 75).
  • Functionally equivalent to Detrimental magic effects -X%.
  • To convert it to Detrimental magic effects -X%, subtract 100 from X.
    • Example: If the tooltip shows Reduction of Detrimental Effects +35.6%, the equivalent is Detrimental magic effects -64.3% (0.1 less due to rounding).
    • Note that it shares the same progression as the Evade Critical Hits -X% bonus.
  • All sources of Detrimental magic effects -X%, including this one, stack by summing their base values (before diminishing returns), with the diminishing returns formula applied once to the total.

Additional notes

  • Detrimental magic effects -X% is a powerful bonus that reduces the duration of secondary effects, debuffs, and spell damage-over-time (DoT).
    • For Burn, Poison, and spell DoT, the reduced duration also means less total damage taken.
    • For Weaken and Freeze, it reduces both the duration and the severity of their penalties (to attributes and run speed, respectively).
  • The only known exception is Stun, which is governed by a separate mechanic and is unaffected.
  • The skill Spell Resistance (at mastery) is the largest single source of this bonus.
