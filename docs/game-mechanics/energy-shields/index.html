Energy Shields

⚠️ Work in progress

Hitpoints

MaxHP = BaseHP * 0.001 * FLOOR(BONUS_SHIELD * (1 + BONUS_SHIELD_REL * 0.001)) * (1 + WardingEnergyLoreSV * 0.001) * (1 + Willpower * WillpowerMultiplier)

Where:

  • BaseHP - Base Hitpoints.
  • BONUS_SHIELD is the total value for that bonus. It is granted by combat arts like Warding Energy (Buff), Divine Protection and T-Energy Shroud (Buff).
  • BONUS_SHIELD_REL is the total value for that bonus (Max. shield energy +X%).
  • WardingEnergyLoreSV is the SkillValue for the given skill level of Warding Energy Lore.
  • WillpowerMultiplier - Hitpoint Bonus from Attributes.
