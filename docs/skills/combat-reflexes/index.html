Combat Reflexes

Increases the chance to evade hostile weapon attacks and reduces the chance to sustain critical hits.

  • Evade Chance +X%
  • Chance Against Critical Weapon Hits -X%

At Level 75: Combat Reflex Mastery

Further increases the chance to evade attacks.

Combat Reflex Mastery

Further increases the chance to evade hostile weapon attacks and lowers the chance to sustain critical hits.

  • Evade Chance +X%
  • Chance Against Critical Weapon Hits -X%

Skill bonuses

Evade Chance +X%

  • Reduces the opponent's chance to hit with weapon attacks by the given percentage. This is a relative reduction.
    • Example: If the base chance to hit is 80%, and the bonus is -50%, then the chance is reduced to 40%.
  • It looks similar to Chance to evade +X%, but it is actually a separate multiplier in the chance to hit formula.
  • Receives a significant one-time increase upon reaching mastery, in addition to the normal progression.

Chance Against Critical Weapon Hits -X%

  • Requires skill mastery (unlocked at skill level 75).
    • Contrary to what the tooltip states, it will not work before mastery.
  • Reduces the chance to sustain a critical hit from any source, including from spells. This is a relative reduction.
    • Example: If the base chance to hit is 80%, and the bonus is -50%, then the chance is reduced to 40%.
