Jolting Touch

The Temple Guardian clutches his opponent with the left hand immobilizing him and sending out electrical impulses for several seconds. Additionally, he slightly recharges his combat arts while Jolting Touch is active.

Modification details

Payback

Chance to reflect close combat damage.

Life Leech

Each impulse leeches life from the target and adds it to the caster's life.

Self Repair

Adds a minor self healing effect to every impulse.

Voltage

Each impulse increases combat art regeneration.

Shelter

While Jolting Touch is active, incoming damage is reduced.

Flux

Increases Jolting Touch's pulse frequency.
