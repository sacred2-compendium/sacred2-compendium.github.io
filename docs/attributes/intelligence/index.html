Intelligence

Intelligence

Increases the chance that a spell hits the target with full force. Magic staffs and spells will additionally increase the damage output.

  • Weapon Damage +X
  • Combat Art Damage X
  • Spell Intensity X

Attribute Bonuses

Weapon Damage +X

  • Intelligence-based weapons gain bonus damage from Intelligence.
  • For details, see Weapon Damage Bonus from Attributes.

Combat Art Damage X

  • Increases spell damage and spell damage-over-time (DoT) of combat arts.
  • Does not affect weapon damage. In this case, the listed value mirrors the Weapon Damage +X bonus.
  • For details, see Spell Damage Bonus from Intelligence

Spell Intensity X

  • Increases the character's base Spell Intensity by Intelligence * 1.

Chance for critical hits +X%

  • Not shown in the attribute tooltip, but confirmed via testing.
  • Affects all combat arts influenced by Aspect Lore.
  • Approximately every 100 points of Intelligence increases the chance for Critical Hits by 1%.
  • Stacks additively with other sources of Chance for critical hits +X%.
