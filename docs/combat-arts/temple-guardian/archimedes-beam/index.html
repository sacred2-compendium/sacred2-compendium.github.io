Modification details

Paralyze

Bundle

Weaken

Significant chance to decrease the target's attributes.

Intent

Chance to land a critical hit.

Shelter

While Archimedes Beam is active, incoming damage is reduced.

Annul

Considerable chance to dispel beneficial spells (buffs) from the target.
