Modification details

Perforate

Chance to cause open wounds (damage over time).

Envenom

Some of the damage will be converted into poison damage.

Breach

Chance that enemy armor value will be ignored, and that full damage will be inflicted.

Trail

Projectiles will home in on the target.

Blast

Chance that an explosion will occur when hitting the target.

Double Shot

Two shots in quick succession.
