Modification details

Hypnosis

Attracts opponents to the center of the pillar.

Delude

Reduces opponents' attack values within the area of effect.

Brightness

Increases the area of effect.

Conserve

Increases duration of Radiant Pillar.

Magnify

Increases damage of Radiant Pillar.

Ordeal

Inflicts twice as much damage when the opponent's health has fallen below a threshold value.
