Concentration

Decreases the regeneration time of all combat arts. Also grants the ability to maintain up to two buffs simultaneously.

  • Regeneration Time -X%
  • Active Buffs 2

At Level 75: Concentration Mastery

Significantly decreases the regeneration time of all combat arts. Also grants the ability to maintain up to three buffs simultaneously.

Concentration Mastery

Significantly decreases the regeneration time of all combat arts. Also grants the ability to maintain up to three buffs simultaneously.

  • Regeneration Time -X%
  • Active Buffs 3

Skill bonuses

Regeneration Time -X%

  • Decreases the regeneration time of all combat arts.
  • Reduces the regeneration time penalty imposed by all buffs.
  • The regeneration time bonuses from Concentration and Aspect Focus are combined into a single multiplier, which affects both combat art regeneration time and buff penalties.
  • The multiplier is derived by summing the base skill values (before diminishing returns), and applying the diminishing returns formula once to the total.

Active Buffs X

  • Characters begin with only one active buff slot.
  • Upon learning the skill, a second buff slot becomes available.
  • Upon mastering the skill, a third and final buff slot becomes available.

Additional notes

  • While Ancient Magic, Combat Discipline, and Concentration are classified as "combat art skills" in the game files and UI, the bonus Combat Art Skills +X does not increase their skill levels.
