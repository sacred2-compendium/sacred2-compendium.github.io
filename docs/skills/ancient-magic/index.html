Ancient Magic Mastery

Increases damage of non-weapon based combat arts and reduces the opponent's magic resistance. Additionally, the opponents' immunities against your combat arts will be reduced.

  • Damage +X%
  • Opponents' Immunities -X%

Skill bonuses

Damage +X%

  • Increases spell damage and spell damage-over-time (DoT) of combat arts. Weapon damage is unaffected.
  • Stacks additively with other percentage-based damage bonuses.

Chance to overcome spell resistance +X%

  • Not shown in the skill tooltip, but confirmed via testing. Name is unofficial, but reflects observed behavior.
  • Has the same progression as the Damage +X% bonus.
  • Increases the chance to succeed at the spell intensity vs spell resistance check by the given percentage. This is a relative increase.
    • Example: If the base chance to succeed is 40%, and the bonus is +100%, then the chance increases to 80%.
  • Stacks additively with the corresponding bonus from the relevant Aspect Focus skill.

Opponents' Immunities -X%

  • Requires skill mastery (unlocked at skill level 75).
  • Affects basic attacks and weapon-based combat arts, in addition to spells.
  • Reduces the target's Damage Mitigation +X% by the given percentage. This is a relative reduction.
    • Example: If an opponent has 80% mitigation and the bonus is -50%, their mitigation is reduced to 40%.
  • If an attack is reflected, the attacker's own Opponents' Immunities -X% bonus is applied against them.
  • Does not affect Armor, Damage over Time: Element -X%, or Sensitivity: Element +X%.

Additional notes

  • While Ancient Magic, Combat Discipline, and Concentration are classified as "combat art skills" in the game files and UI, the bonus Combat Art Skills +X does not increase their skill levels.
