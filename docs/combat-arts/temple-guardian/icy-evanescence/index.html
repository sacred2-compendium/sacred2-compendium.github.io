Icy Evanescence

Causes ice damage and decreases opponents' ice resistance within a certain radius. Allied players receive a bonus to fire resistance. If the combat art "Fiery Ember" is active within range, the spell with the higher level will cancel the weaker one.

Modification details

Icy Needles

Increases ice damage within range.

Freeze

Slows down opponents' movement rate.

Fire Protection

Reduces fire damage even more.

Deep Frost

Decreases opponents' resistance against ice damage.

Energy Efficiency

Decreases the regeneration time.

Sustain

Increases the duration of Icy Evanescence.
