Description

The Hitpoint Regeneration +X/s bonus is non-functional. For some reason it doesn't work on this combat art.

Modification details

Distract

Reduces opponents' attack speed within the area of effect.

Blind

Reduces opponents' defense values within the area of effect.

Purify

Increases damage against undead creatures.

Cleanse

Increases fire damage against T-mutations.

Eagerness

Increases damage per second.

Illuminate

Increases the area of effect.
