Influence of Willpower on Secondary Effects

⚠️ Work in progress

After reaching a certain threshold, increasing Willpower begins to shorten the duration of the secondary effects Burn and Poison. By reducing the duration, the total damage is reduced as well. The threshold itself rises as the character's level increases.

Threshold = (CharacterLevel + 9) * EffectWillpower * 0.1

Where:

  • EffectWillpower is parameter from balance.txt. The default value in the unmodded game is 25.

Multiplier = MIN(1, Threshold / MAX(1, Willpower))
