Modification details

War Song

The battle cry intensifies and increases the attack values even more.

Persistence

Prolongs the duration of Rousing Command.

Spur

Increases run speed.

Impulse

Increases also attack and cast speed.

Surge

The regeneration time of all combat arts is decreased while Rousing Command is active.

Leader

Rousing Command will also affect nearby party members.
