Tactics Lore

Increases damage dealt by equipped weapons as well as the chance for critical hits. Enables the use of modifications for weapon-based combat arts.

  • Damage +X%
  • Chance for Critical Hits Increased by +X%

At Level 75: Tactics Mastery

Further increases the chance for critical hits.

Tactics Mastery

Increases damage dealt by equipped weapons. Greatly increases the chance to land critical hits. Enables the use of modifications for weapon-based combat arts.

  • Damage +X%
  • Chance for Critical Hits Increased by +X%

Skill bonuses

Modification Point Contribution

  • Investing skill points in this skill contributes toward earning modification points for all aspects influenced by Tactics Lore.

Damage +X%

  • Affects basic attacks and all combat arts influenced by Tactics Lore.
  • Stacks additively with other percentage-based damage bonuses.

Additional effects on Combat Arts

  • The skill increases the base values of certain combat art bonuses by the same percentage as the Damage +X% bonus.
  • Callous Execution
    • Increases the Life leeched per hit +X bonus of the Draw Life modification.
  • Ruthless Mutilation
    • Increases the Life leeched per hit +X bonus of the Dolor modification.
  • Demonic Blow
    • Increases the Life leeched per hit +X bonus of the Life Leech modification.
  • Grim Resilience (Buff)
    • Increases the Maximum Hitpoints +X bonus, including the bonus of the Fortify modification.
    • Increases the Hitpoint Regeneration +X/s bonus of the Rejuvenation modification.
  • Frenzied Rampage
    • Increases the Life leeched per hit +X bonus of the Vampire modification.
  • Augmenting Guidon
    • Increases the Hitpoint Regeneration +X/s bonus of the Healing modification.
  • Combat Alert
    • Increases the Hitpoint Regeneration +X/s bonus of the Repair modification.
  • Battle Aura (Buff)
    • Increases the Hitpoint Regeneration +X/s bonus of the Repair modification.
  • T-Energy Shroud (Buff)
    • Increases the Block Warding Energy +X bonus.
    • Increases the energy shield hitpoint regeneration bonus of the Recharge modification.

Chance for Critical Hits Increased by +X%

  • Affects basic attacks and all combat arts influenced by Tactics Lore.
  • The value of X increases with skill level but decreases with character level, leading to diminishing returns at higher character levels.
  • Stacks additively with other sources of Chance for critical hits +X%.

Additional notes

  • Some of the combat arts influenced by Tactics Lore deal spell damage, like Belligerent Vault, Deathly Spears, Dust Devil, Killing Spree and T-Energy Shroud (Buff). Those are affected by Tactics Lore as well.
  • Other aspects also contain weapon-based combat arts, like Archangel's Wrath, Magic Coup and Spectral Hand. However, these are not affected by Tactics Lore.
  • Equipping a weapon is not necessary to benefit from this skill. Unarmed strikes are affected as well.
