Modification details

Greek Fire

Increases the chance that the hit opponent will burn.

Explosion

Fireballs inflict area effect damage on nearby opponents.

Fusillade

Conjures an additional fireball. Overall damage will be increased but divided between the fireballs.

Globe

The fireball increases in power and inflicts more damage.

Hot Pursuit

Fireballs will follow their targets.
