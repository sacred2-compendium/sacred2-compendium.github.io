Crystal Skin (Buff)

Improves armor rating and resistance against spells. Slightly increases the regeneration time of all combat arts within the aspect Mystic Stormite while the buff is active. Crystal Skin cannot be used in conjunction with Incandescent Skin.

Modification details

Freeze

Slows movement rate of opponents in close combat range.

Glacial Mirror

Increases the chance to reflect incoming detrimental magical effects.

Feel Cold

Reduces the opponent's resistance against ice damage.

Focalize

Increases the spell intensity of all aspects.

Mystic Stormite Expertise

Decreases the regeneration time for the Mystic Stormite aspect.

Frosty Breeze

Additionally increases ice damage.
