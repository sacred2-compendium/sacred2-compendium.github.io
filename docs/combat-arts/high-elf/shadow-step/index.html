Modification details

Phase Shift

Decreases the cooldown of Shadow Step.

Escape

Chance to break all rooting effects while teleporting.

Vanish

The caster remains invisible for a brief period after the teleport.

Sanctuary

The caster is briefly invulnerable from physical damage after the teleport.

Explosion

Inflicts area damage while teleporting.

Mend

Heals the caster during teleport.
