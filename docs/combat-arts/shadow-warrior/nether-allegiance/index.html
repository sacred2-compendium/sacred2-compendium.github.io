Modification details

Squad

Conjures an additional spectral soldier.

Sharp Blades

The spectral soldiers inflict more damage.

Commander

Conjures yet another spectral soldier.

Tower Shield

Equips the conjured spectral soldiers with significantly improved shields.

Refreshment

Increases the hitpoint regeneration rate of the spectral soldiers.

Elite Equipment

Enhances the spectral soldiers' armor.
