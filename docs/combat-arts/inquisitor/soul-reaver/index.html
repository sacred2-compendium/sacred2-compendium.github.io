Soul Reaver (Buff)

Raises the souls of fallen corpses. These will surround the Inquisitor for a while before they disappear again. Each fresh soul provides an additional boost to the Inquisitor's attack and defense, as well as resetting the souls' lifespan.

Modification details

Deathmagic

Souls inflict magic damage to nearby opponents.

Zealot

Increases the lifespan of each reanimated soul.

Recreation

Increases the Inquisitor's hitpoint regeneration rate while he is accompanied by souls.

Source

Decreases the regeneration time of combat arts while the Inquisitor is accompanied by souls.

Soul Imbiber

Heals the Inquisitor every time he collects a soul.
