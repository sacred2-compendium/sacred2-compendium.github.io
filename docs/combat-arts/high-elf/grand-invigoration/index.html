Modification details

Arrant Pyromancer Expertise

The regeneration time for the aspect Arrant Pyromancer will decrease significantly.

Mystic Stormite Expertise

The regeneration time for the aspect Mystic Stormite will decrease significantly.

Replenish

Further decreases the regeneration time of all spells.

Life Energy

Additionally increases the hitpoint regeneration rate.

Resilience

Reduces the impact and duration of incoming detrimental effects.

Fleetness

Increases run speed while Grand Invigoration is active.
