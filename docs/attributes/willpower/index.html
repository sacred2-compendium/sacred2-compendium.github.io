Willpower

Increases resistance against magic spells and additional damage effects (burn, poison, freeze, weaken).

  • Spell Resistance X

Overview

Characters start with a base amount of Willpower and gain additional Willpower as they level up. Willpower can also be increased by investing attribute points or through equipment bonuses. The survival bonus can increase all attributes by up to 33.33%.

Attribute Bonuses

Spell Resistance X

  • Increases the character's base Spell Resistance by Willpower * 1.

Energy Shield Hitpoints +X

  • Not shown in the attribute tooltip, but confirmed via testing.
  • Increases maximum energy shield hitpoints by a percentage of the character's base energy shield hitpoints.
  • X expresses this increase in absolute terms and varies as the character's level and base hitpoints increase.

Each point of Willpower increases the character's base energy shield hitpoints by a percentage. This percentage decreases as the character levels up.
