Modification details

Porous

Reduces the opponent's resistance to physical damage.

Headhunter

Increases the chance to receive a shrunken head when the victim dies.

Elongate

Increases duration of Black Curse.

Distress

Reduces opponent's attack speed.

Expert

Reduces the regeneration time of Black Curse.

Dispel

Deactivates the target's permanent beneficial spells.
