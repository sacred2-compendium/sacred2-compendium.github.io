Modification details

Spell Flow

Significantly decreases the regeneration time of Frost Flare.

Freeze

Further reduces target's movement rate.

Constraint

Reduces the opponent's chance to hit successfully.

Frost

Additionally increases the ice damage over time.

Icy Circle

Freezes opponents within a significantly larger area around the target.

Perpetuity

Increases the duration of the freezing effects.
