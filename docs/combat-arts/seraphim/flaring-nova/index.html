Modification details

Expand

Increases Flaring Nova's radius.

Impulse

Sends out two pulses, but reduces strength and area of effect.

Perplex

Opponents temporarily lose their attack power.

Stun

Chance to stun opponents within range.

Flux

Increases Flaring Nova's damage.

Laggard

Slows the target down.
