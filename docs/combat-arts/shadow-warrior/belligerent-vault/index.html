Modification details

Reach

Extends the range of Belligerent Vault.

Routine

Decreases the regeneration time for Belligerent Vault.

Unaware

Reduces the opponent's defense value.

Shock

Opponents at the starting location of the jump become confused which slows them down.

Commotion

Chance to briefly stun opponents in the designated area when the Shadow Warrior lands.

Unbound

Good chance to escape rooting effects.
