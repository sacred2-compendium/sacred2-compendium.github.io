Strength

Strength

Increases attack value as well as basic damage for all close range weapons except daggers and mage staffs.

  • Attack Value +X
  • Weapon Damage +X

Overview

Characters start with a base amount of Strength and gain additional Strength as they level up. Strength can also be increased by investing attribute points or through equipment bonuses. The survival bonus can increase all attributes by up to 33.33%.

Attribute Bonuses

Attack Value +X

  • Increases the character's base Attack Value by FLOOR(Strength * 0.8).

Weapon Damage +X

  • Strength-based weapons and unarmed strikes gain bonus damage from Strength.
  • For details, see Weapon Damage Bonus from Attributes.
