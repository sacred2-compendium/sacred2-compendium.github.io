Modification details

Anxiety

Chance that hit opponents will run away.

Solid

Increased duration of Malicious Totem.

Pointed

Arrows inflict more damage.

Venomous

Increases poison damage on arrows.

Expert

Reduces the cooldown of Malicious Totem.

Rapid Fire

Increases the totem's firing rate.
