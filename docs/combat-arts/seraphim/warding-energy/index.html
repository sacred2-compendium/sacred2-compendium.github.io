Modification details

Field Force

Strengthens the energy field and absorbs more damage until it is exhausted.

Projectile Reflection

Magic Mirror

Chance to reflect spell damage.

Block

Blocks a fixed amount of damage from every successful hit.

Resource

Reduces the cost to maintain the shield.
