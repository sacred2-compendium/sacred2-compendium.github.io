Modification details

Armament

The animated corpses will receive an armor bonus.

Dark Realm

Increases the effective range of the conjuration.

Vivification

The animated corpses live considerably longer.

Demand

Chance to animate an increased number of corpses.

Contagion

Adds a chance for the animated corpses to infect their victims, turning them into undead servants.

Furor

Animated corpses inflict more damage.
