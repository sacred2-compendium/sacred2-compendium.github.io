Modification details

Double Attack

Chance to strike twice with one hit.

Wounding

Chance that a successful hit will leave the opponent with an open wound (damage over time).

Double Strike

Increases the chance for a double attack.

Intent

Chance to land critical hits.

Envenom

Adds poison damage to Frenzied Rampage.

Vampire

Chance for the Shadow Warrior to leech hitpoints from the target and add them to his own hitpoints.
