Viperish Disease

Infects the victim with harmful poison. The lower the target's hitpoints, the more damage Viperish Disease will inflict. If the Dryad has a shrunken head equipped, opponents that correspond to the shrunken head will be more likely to catch the disease from the target.

Modification details

Gangrene

Targets do not need to be as severely injured before the damage output of Viperish Disease increases.

Relapse

Increases the duration of Viperish Disease which leaves more time for opponents to become infected.

Transmission

Increases the chance to transmit the disease even with no shrunken head equipped.

Headhunter

Increases the chance to receive a shrunken head when the victim dies.

Gouge

Chance of inflicting a deep wound on first target.

Contaminate

Extends the range of Viperish Disease.
