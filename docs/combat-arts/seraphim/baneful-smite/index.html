Modification details

Laggard

Slows the target down.

Disarm

Opponent temporarily loses his weapon.

Potential

Increases the chance that the damage will have a lasting effect on the target.

Confuse

Reduces the target's attack value.

Electrocute

The magic damage will definitely induce its secondary effect.

Chain

Chance for chain lightning.
