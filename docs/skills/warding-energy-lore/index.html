Consumable Trophies

  • Enables the use of consumable trophies governed by Warding Energy Lore.

Item Bonuses Governed By Skill

  • Low- and mid-tier armor pieces commonly feature item bonuses such as Max. shield energy (Warding Energy Lore) or Absorbtion warding energy (Warding Energy Lore).
  • These bonuses remain locked until the governing skill is learned or mastered, as specified by each bonus.
  • Unlike regular item bonuses, skill-governed bonuses scale with the current skill level rather than item level, reaching maximum potential at skill level 250.
  • Additionally, while regular item bonuses use the item's difficulty level to determine their value range, skill-governed bonuses instead use the difficulty level of the current game session.
  • As a result, these bonuses adjust dynamically based on both the current skill level and difficulty level.

Skill bonuses

Shield Energy +X%

  • Increases maximum energy shield hitpoints by a percentage of the energy shield's base hitpoints.
  • Stacks additively with other sources of Max. shield energy +X%.

Damage Absorption +X

  • Functionally equivalent to Block Warding Energy +X.
  • Reduces damage received by a fixed amount. This reduction happens after almost everything else, but before Death Blow and the level difference bonus/penalty.
  • Does not affect damage-over-time (DoT) of any kind.
  • Stacks additively with other sources of Block Warding Energy +X.

Shield Regeneration +X%

  • This bonus is non-functional. It has no effect on the shield regeneration rate (in or out of combat) or shield regeneration delay.
