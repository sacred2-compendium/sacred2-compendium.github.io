Modification details

Reload

Increases the number of projectiles or hits.

Edged

Increases damage dealt by Darting Assault.

Throwback

Chance to knock opponents back slightly.

Wounding

Chance that a successful hit will leave the opponent with an open wound (damage over time).

Rotate

The attack is directed at opponents all around the Dryad.

Pierce

Chance that a projectile continues on its path after a hit.
