Modification details

Rebound

Adds a chance to reflect magic damage.

Sphere

Opponents within a certain radius around the Inquisitor will be hit and damaged by small streaks of lightning.

Counterblow

Adds a chance to reflect close combat damage.

Perfection

Increases the effectiveness against ranged damage.

Exploit

Each party member receives 5% of the damage intended for the Inquisitor.

Evade

Increases the defense value.
