Modification details

Bloodlust

Further increases attack and cast speed.

Sprint

Further increases run speed.

Reprisal

Increases the Seraphim's damage output when she is injured.

Delay

Increases the duration of Dashing Alacrity.

Ardor

Decreases the regeneration time of all aspects.

Impatience

Reduces the regeneration time of Dashing Alacrity.
