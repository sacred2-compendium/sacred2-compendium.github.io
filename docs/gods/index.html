Gods

Lumen

Lumen is the God of Light. He makes all things visible. He is a healer and knows what has been and what will be.

Dazzle

Divine lightning blinds all those who are near. Dazed by its glistening light they are briefly unable to move. The effect is even more severe against some powers of evil. They will combust instantly and burn in the cleansing light of Lumen!

Forens

Forens is the Goddess of Philosophy and Wisdom.

Inspiration

Foren's divine wisdom fills the hero with inspiration and insight. For a brief period all damage taken will be reflected back to the attacker who consequently harms himself.

Kybele

Kybele is the Goddess of Nature — she is the patron of fertility and life, of flora and fauna.

Infusion

Kybele's healing hand blesses all allied creatures. Their wounds disappear, their pain vanishes. For a brief period their souls are strengthened by Kybele's divine blessing so that all attacks against them fail.

Testa

Testa is the God of Science. He is the spirit who brings life to dead and mechanical things.

Will-o-Wisp

Several will—o—wisps appear out of nowhere. Animated by Testa's spirit they assist the hero by healing his wounds and absorbing incoming damage.

Kuan

Kuan is the God of War. He protects the soldiers and grants them the determination necessary to persist in battle.

Kuan's Breath

All creatures on the battlefield feel Kuan's Breath. Their recklessness causes them to kill everything in their path. Friend or foe, there will be no distinction — they all will be annihilated.

Ker

Ker is the Goddess of Evil. She is the destroyer, and the guardian of darkness.

Sakkara Demon

From depths of the dimensions Ker conjures a demon which will assist the caster in his fight for evil until it is either destroyed or succeeds in casting off its magic shackles thus breaking free from the conjurer's control.
