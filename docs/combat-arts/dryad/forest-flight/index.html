Modification details

Recovery

Heals the Dryad slightly during her Forest Flight movement.

Roots

Increases the chance to root nearby enemies.

Expert

Decreases the cooldown of Forest Flight.

Escape

Increases the chance to break immobilizing spells.

Refresh

Adds a light healing effect while moving.
