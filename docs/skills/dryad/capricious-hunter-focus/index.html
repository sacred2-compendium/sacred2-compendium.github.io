Capricious Hunter Focus

Capricious Hunter Focus

Decreases the regeneration time for the aspect Capricious Hunter and increases the maximum spell level without penalty. Enables the use of modifications for this aspect.

  • Regeneration Time -X%
  • Highest Spell Level without Penalty X

At Level 75: Capricious Hunter Mastery

Further decreases the regeneration time. Spell levels are raised even higher without penalty.

Capricious Hunter Mastery

Significantly decreases the regeneration time for the aspect Capricious Hunter and increases the maximum spell level without penalty. Enables the use of modifications for this aspect.

  • Regeneration Time -X%
  • Highest Spell Level without Penalty X

Skill bonuses

Modification Point Contribution

  • Investing skill points in this skill contributes toward earning modification points for the aspect Capricious Hunter.

Regeneration Time -X%

  • Affects all combat arts associated with the aspect Capricious Hunter.
  • Decreases the regeneration time of combat arts.
  • Reduces the regeneration time penalty imposed by buffs.
  • The regeneration time bonuses from Concentration and Aspect Focus are combined into a single multiplier, which affects both combat art regeneration time and buff penalties.
  • The multiplier is derived by summing the base skill values (before diminishing returns), and applying the diminishing returns formula once to the total.

Highest Spell Level without Penalty X

  • Increases the highest combat art level without penalty for the aspect Capricious Hunter. Refer to the progression table below.

Chance to overcome spell resistance +X%

  • Not shown in the skill tooltip, but confirmed via testing. Name is unofficial, but reflects observed behavior.
  • X can be calculated using a formula. See the progression table below.
  • Affects all combat arts associated with the aspect Capricious Hunter.
  • Increases the chance to succeed at the spell intensity vs spell resistance check by the given percentage. This is a relative increase.
    • Example: If the base chance to succeed is 40%, and the bonus is +100%, then the chance increases to 80%.
  • Stacks additively with the corresponding bonus from Ancient Magic.
