Modification details

Strong Voice

Increases the area of effect.

Elocution

Increases the chance to convert at least two opponents.

Sinner

At least one opponent will attack the converted target.

Preacher

Decreases the regeneration time of Instill Belief.

Blessing

Restores health of freshly converted targets.

Fanatic

Increases the attack speed of converted targets.
