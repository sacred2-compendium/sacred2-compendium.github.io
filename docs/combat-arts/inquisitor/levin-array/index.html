Modification details

Disperse

Increases the angle of effectiveness.

Elongation

Increases the range of the lightning streaks.

Paralyze

Briefly decreases movement speed of affected opponents.

Hesitation

Briefly reduces the opponent's attack speed.

Steal Life

Adds a chance to draw life from opponents.

Dynamic

Increases the chance for critical hits.
