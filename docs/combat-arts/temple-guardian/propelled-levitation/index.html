Modification details

Capacitor

Decreases the regeneration time of Propelled Levitation.

Super Charger

Increases damage range.

Boost

Propelled Levitation will cover longer distances.

Jet Stream

Opponents within range sustain additional fire damage.

Emergency Lift-off

Enables lift-off even when under the influence of immobilizing spells.

Flux

Increases the jet nozzles' pulse frequency.
