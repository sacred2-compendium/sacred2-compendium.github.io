Nefarious Netherworld Lore

Increases inflicted damage, critical hit chance and execution speed of the aspect Nefarious Netherworld. Enables the use of modifications for this aspect.

  • Damage +X%
  • Chance for Critical Hit +X%
  • Execution Speed +X%

At Level 75: Nefarious Netherworld Proficiency

Further increases inflicted damage.

Nefarious Netherworld Proficiency

Significantly increases inflicted damage, critical hit chance and execution speed of the aspect Nefarious Netherworld. Enables the use of modifications for this aspect.

  • Damage +X%
  • Chance for Critical Hit +X%
  • Execution Speed +X%

Skill bonuses

Modification Point Contribution

  • Investing skill points in this skill contributes toward earning modification points for the aspect Celestial Magic.

Damage +X%

  • Affects all combat arts associated with the aspect Celestial Magic.
  • Stacks additively with other percentage-based damage bonuses.

Additional effects on Combat Arts

  • The skill increases the base values of certain combat art bonuses by the same percentage as the Damage +X% bonus.
  • Soul Reaver (Buff)
    • Increases the Hitpoint Regeneration +X/s bonus of the Recreation modification.
  • Eruptive Desecration
    • Increases the poison DoT of the Plague modification.

Chance for Critical Hit +X%

  • Affects all combat arts associated with the aspect Celestial Magic.
  • The value of X increases with skill level but decreases with character level, leading to diminishing returns at higher character levels.
  • Stacks additively with other sources of Chance for critical hits +X%.

Execution Speed +X%

  • Affects all combat arts associated with the aspect Celestial Magic.
  • Stacks additively with other sources of execution speed, like Casting Speed +X%.
