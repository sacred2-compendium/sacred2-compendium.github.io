Modification details

Creep

Opponents have to get closer to detect the Shadow Warrior while he is walking amongst them with Shadow Veil active.

Avoidance

Reduces the effects of incoming detrimental spells.

Nimble

Increases run speed while Shadow Veil is active.

Fade

Greatly reduces the detection radius while casting spells.

Sinister Pact

The effect of Shadow Veil is permanent rather than temporary.

Shadowmaster

Significantly reduces the cooldown of Shadow Veil.
