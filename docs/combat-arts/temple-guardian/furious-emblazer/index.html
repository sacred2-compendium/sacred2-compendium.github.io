Modification details

Accelerant

Furious Emblazer inflicts more fire damage.

Immolation

Considerable chance that victims will sustain additional fire damage over time.

Terror

Chance that hit opponents will run away.

Incapacitate

Chance to briefly stun the target.

Intensify

Increases the damage output of Furious Emblazer.

Burnout

Increases damage against severely injured opponents.
