Modification details

Spurt

Extends the range of Assailing Somersault.

Athletic

Decreases the regeneration time of Assailing Somersault.

Brawn

Increases weapon damage.

Impetus

Inflicts damage not only on the target but also on nearby opponents.

Bewilder

The Seraphim leaps away briefly reducing the run speed of opponents at her starting location.

Concussion

Chance to stun the target.
