Modification details

Gravitation

Adds a damage bonus to Clustering Maelstrom.

Chaos

Opponents tumbling into other opponents will inflict more damage.

Vortex

Increases the range of Clustering Maelstrom.

Gash

Adds bleeding damage (damage over time).

Stupefy

Adds a chance to stun opponents briefly.
