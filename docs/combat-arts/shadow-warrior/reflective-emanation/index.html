Modification details

Tough

Adds a chance to reflect stun effects.

Backlash

Adds a chance to also reflect ranged attacks.

Unstoppable

Adds a chance to reflect root effects.

Antimagic

Adds a chance to reflect incoming spells.

Riposte

Adds a chance to increase the reflected damage.

Idol

Extends the reflective effect to nearby party members.
