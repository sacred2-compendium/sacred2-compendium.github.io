Description

This combat art is a buff. It can be toggled on or off at any time. Each activated buff increases the regeneration time of all combat arts by applying its penalty.

Upon activation, the character will perform a lengthy casting animation. The animation speed depends on the character's Execution Speed for the aspect Revered Technology.

At the end of the animation, the BeeEffGee (weapon) will appear in an empty weapon slot and the character will automatically switch to that slot.

  • If there are no empty weapon slots, then the activation will fail.

⚠️ Important: Always switch to an empty weapon slot before summoning the weapon. This allows the damage bonus from both Enhancement modifications to be added directly to the weapon's base damage, which in turn results in a significantly higher damage bonus from Dexterity.

Deactivating the buff will make the weapon disappear.

The following bonuses of this combat art are transferred over to the weapon itself:

  • The Chance to pierce +X% bonus.
  • The Damage +X bonus from both the Enhancement modifications.
  • The Chance that opponents cannot evade attacks +X% bonus from the Accuracy modification.
  • The Chance to pierce +X% bonus from the Penetrate modification.

The following bonuses apply to the character as a whole (not tied to the weapon):

  • The Attack Value +X% bonus.
  • The Ranged Weapons +X bonus.

If you save and exit the game while the character is wielding the BeeEffGee, they will still appear to be wielding it in the game menus. However, since buffs always start deactivated when loading a game, the character will end up being unarmed.

Modification details

Enhancement

Inflicts more damage.

Adds 8 + 2 * Level physical damage to the weapon.

When the weapon is summoned while the currently selected weapon slot is empty, then the extra damage is added directly to the weapon's base damage. This, in turn, results in a significantly higher damage bonus from Dexterity.

This bonus is boosted by Revered Technology Lore's Damage +X% bonus, before adding it to the weapon.

Battery

Reduces the cost to maintain the BeeEffGee.

Reduces the regeneration time penalty of this buff only. This bonus represents an independent multiplier.

The formula is:

ValueOfBonus = FLOOR(5 * Level) + 500

DiminishedValue = 1000 * ValueOfBonus / (1000 + ValueOfBonus)

Percentage = DiminishedValue / 10

Accuracy

Increases the chance to hit.

Penetrate

Chance to pierce through targets.

Increases the Chance to pierce +X%. This stacks additively with the base chance.
