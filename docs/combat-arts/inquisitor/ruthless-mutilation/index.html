Modification details

Deprivation

Even further reduces movement speed of affected opponents.

Ire

Increases the damage inflicted upon all affected opponents.

Dolor

Adds a chance to draw life from the target opponent.

Petrify

Adds a chance to stun opponents briefly.

Smolder

Adds fire damage to each successful hit.

Frenzy

Chance for double hits on each affected opponent.
