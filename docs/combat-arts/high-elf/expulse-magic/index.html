Modification details

Strength

Increases banishing potential but maintains the same effective radius.

Expansion

Increases effective radius but maintains the banishing potential.

Pentagram

Reduces the duration and impact of incoming weakening effects.

Banish Dead

Damages undead creatures within the radius of effectiveness.

Protection

Chance to block direct magical damage.
