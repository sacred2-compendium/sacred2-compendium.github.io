Toughness

Adds a base resistance for each type of damage and reduces incoming damage.

  • Armor per Damage Type +X
  • Opponent's Damage -X%

At Level 75: Toughness Mastery

Significantly improved damage reduction.

Toughness Mastery

Adds a base resistance for each type of damage and greatly reduces incoming damage.

  • Armor per Damage Type +X
  • Opponent's Damage -X%

Skill bonuses

Armor per Damage Type +X

  • Increases the character's base Armor for each of the five damage types by the listed amount.
  • Stacks additively with other sources of Armor +X.

Opponent's Damage -X%

  • Functionally equivalent to Damage Mitigation +X%.
  • To convert it to Damage Mitigation +X%, invert the sign of X.
    • Example: If the tooltip shows Opponent's Damage -12.0%, the equivalent is Damage Mitigation +12.0%.
  • Stacks additively with other sources of Damage Mitigation +X%.
