Batteries (SUBFAM_WEAPON_BATTERY)

Batteries do not have their own weapon skill. Instead, their Attack Value and Attack Speed are based on the character's currently equipped primary weapon. Any weapon skill that modifies the primary weapon will therefore indirectly improve battery performance.

The attack speed penalty imposed by higher-level batteries is non-functional.

Unlike primary weapons, batteries do not receive a damage bonus from character attributes, which significantly limits their overall damage potential.
