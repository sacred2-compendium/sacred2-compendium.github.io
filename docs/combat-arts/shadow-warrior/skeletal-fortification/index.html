Modification details

Rapid Fire

Increases the skeletal tower's firing rate.

Focus

Reduces the cooldown time for the skeletal tower.

Ice Shards

Adds ice damage.

Arcane Power

Adds magic damage.

Scatter Shot

Adds another projectile to the skeletal tower.

Perforate

The arrows pierce through their targets and continue on their flight path.
