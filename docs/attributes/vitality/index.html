Vitality

Increases overall hitpoints and reduces their regeneration time.

Attribute Bonuses

Hitpoints +X

  • Increases maximum life hitpoints by a percentage of base hitpoints.
  • X expresses this increase in absolute terms and varies as the character's level and base hitpoints increase.
  • Stacks additively with the corresponding bonus from Constitution.
  • Compare Vitality and Constitution.

Hitpoint Regeneration X/s

  • The natural regeneration rate is proportional to the maximum hitpoints.
