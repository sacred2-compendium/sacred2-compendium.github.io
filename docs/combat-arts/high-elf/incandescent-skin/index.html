Incandescent Skin (Buff)

Inflicts short range fire damage and offers some resistance against fire attacks. Slightly increases the regeneration time of all combat arts within the aspect Arrant Pyromancer while the buff is active. Incandescent Skin cannot be used in conjunction with Crystal Skin.

Modification details

Inferno

Increases the chance to set fire to opponents when they sustain fire damage.

Combusting Arrows

Chance that arrows may burn before they can inflict damage.

Energy Focus

Decreases the regeneration time of the aspect Arrant Pyromancer.

Revenge

Chance to reflect close combat damage.

Arrant Pyromancer Expertise

Further decreases the regeneration time for the Arrant Pyromancer aspect.

Fiery Ardor

Additionally increases fire damage.
