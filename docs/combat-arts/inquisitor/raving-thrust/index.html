Modification details

Lash Out

Increases the range of Raving Thrust.

Bedaze

Adds a chance to stun opponents briefly.

Relay

Opponents near the targeted opponent will also be knocked back.

Vehemence

Increases damage caused.

Lesion

A successful hit greatly reduces the target's base hitpoints for a brief period.

Tumble

Adds a chance to disarm opponents when they are knocked back.
