Constitution Mastery

Increases maximum hitpoints and increases hitpoint regeneration rate in combat.

  • Hitpoints +X
  • Hitpoint Regeneration +X%

Skill bonuses

Hitpoints +X

  • Increases maximum life hitpoints by a percentage of base hitpoints.
  • X expresses this increase in absolute terms and varies as the character's level and base hitpoints increase.
  • Stacks additively with the corresponding bonus from Vitality.
  • Compare Vitality and Constitution.

Hitpoint Regeneration +X%

  • The natural regeneration rate is proportional to the maximum hitpoints. This bonus reflects the increase in regeneration rate resulting from the Hitpoints +X bonus. It is purely descriptive and non-functional.

Combat Regeneration

  • While in combat, natural hitpoint regeneration is normally reduced by a factor of 50. Upon mastering the skill, this penalty is removed entirely.
  • Creatures (NPC) are not affected by this bonus and will continue to regenerate at the reduced rate.
