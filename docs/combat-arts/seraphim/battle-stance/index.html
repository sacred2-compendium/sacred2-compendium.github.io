Modification details

Premonition

Further increases attack value.

Aggression

Further increases weapon damage output.

Drill

Reduces the energy cost of Battle Stance.

Flexibility

Chance to evade weapon attacks.

Idol

Extends a portion of the effects to party members.

Retaliation

Small chance to reflect close combat damage.
