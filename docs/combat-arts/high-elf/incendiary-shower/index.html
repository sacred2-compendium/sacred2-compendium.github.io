Modification details

Skull Smash

Chance to stun opponents upon impact.

Magma Rain

Increases fire damage of the meteors.

Continuance

Prolongs the duration of Incendiary Shower.

Smoldering Boulder

The meteors inflict more physical damage.

Devastation

Increases the chance for critical hits.

Copious Shower

The meteors rain down faster and with shortened intervals.
