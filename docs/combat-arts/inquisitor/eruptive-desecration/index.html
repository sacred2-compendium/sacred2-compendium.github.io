Modification details

Greed

Adds a chance to receive additional items from the corpse.

Restore

The soul of a corpse will heal the Inquisitor.

Violence

Increases the damage dealt by explosions.

Danger Zone

Increases the damage range of the explosions.

Apocalypse

Increases the explosion's range.

Plague

The explosion has an additional poisonous effect.
