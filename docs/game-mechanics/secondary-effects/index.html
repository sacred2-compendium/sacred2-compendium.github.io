Secondary effects

⚠️ Work in progress

Successful hits that deal damage of a type, other than physical, have a chance to inflict that damage type's secondary effect. This is true for spell damage as well, but not for spell DoT.

  • Magic damage causes Weaken.
  • Fire damage causes Burn.
  • Poison damage causes Poison.
  • Ice damage causes Freeze.

The chance to inflict a secondary effect depends on the proportion of that damage type relative to the total damage, divided by a factor. This factor is different for weapon attacks and for spells. Even if the character has 100% chance to inflict a secondary effect, at least 1 damage of that type must be dealt to the opponent. I don't know if the chance is determined one-sided, i.e. only based on the character's stats, or two-sided, i.e. after the opponent reduces the damage via armor, mitigation, etc.

When reapplying a secondary effect, the duration is reset. In addition, for Burn and Poison, the greater damage between the two applications is used for the new application.

A target can be affected by multiple different secondary effects at the same time, but only one visual effect will be displayed. TODO ORDER.

Weaken

Both the attribute penalty and the duration can be reduced by Detrimental magic effects -X% (including from the skill Spell Resistance Mastery).

Fire

Causes fire damage over time (DoT). The total damage is equal to the actual damage dealt to the opponent, all damage types included. The duration is 5 seconds.

The damage can be reduced by Damage Mitigation +X%, Damage over time -X%, Willpower, level difference bonus/penalty.

It is not affected by armor.

This damage can be increased by the skill Damage Lore Mastery.

The duration can be reduced by Detrimental magic effects -X% (and the skill Spell Resistance Mastery). By reducing the duration, the total damage is reduced as well.

Poison

Causes poison damage over time (DoT). The total damage is equal to the actual damage dealt to the opponent, all damage types included. The duration is 5 seconds.

The damage can be reduced by Damage Mitigation +X%, Damage over time -X%, Willpower, level difference bonus/penalty.

It is not affected by armor.

This damage can be increased by the skill Damage Lore Mastery.

The duration can be reduced by Detrimental magic effects -X% (and the skill Spell Resistance Mastery). By reducing the duration, the total damage is reduced as well.

Freeze
