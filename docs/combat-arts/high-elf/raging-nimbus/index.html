Modification details

Hurricane

Increases the blizzard's damage frequency.

Wrath

Increases the blizzard's ice damage.

Momentum

Further decreases movement speed of opponents caught in blizzard.

Roar

Reduces the attack value of affected opponents.

Cyclone

Increases the duration of the blizzard.

Extended Nimbus

Increases the radius of the blizzard.
