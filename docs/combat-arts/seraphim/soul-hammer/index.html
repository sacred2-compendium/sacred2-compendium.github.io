Modification details

Aim

Increases the chance to land critical hits.

Wounding

Chance to cause an open wound (damage over time).

Battering

Temporarily reduces the target's armor class.

Disarm

Chance to dislodge the weapon from the opponent's hand.

Incision

Chance to inflict a deep wound.

Coup de Grâce

Inflicts twice as much damage when the opponent's health has fallen below a threshold value.
