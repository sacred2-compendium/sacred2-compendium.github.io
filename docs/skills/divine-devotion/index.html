Divine Devotion

Divine Devotion

Decreases the regeneration time of the Divine Gift and unlocks additional attributes of the artifacts.

  • Regeneration Time -X%

At Level 75: Divine Devotion Mastery

Further decreases the regeneration time of the Divine Gift.

Divine Devotion Mastery

Greatly decreases the regeneration time of the Divine Gift and unlocks additional attributes of the artifacts.

  • Regeneration Time -X%

Skill bonuses

Regeneration Time -X%

  • Reduces the cooldown of the character's Divine Gift.

Highest Combat Art Level Without Penalty +X

  • Not shown in the skill tooltip, but confirmed via testing.
  • Increases the highest combat art level without penalty for the character's Divine Gift.

Additional notes

  • God powers use cooldown time instead of regeneration time. That is why they are unaffected by stats and bonuses that decrease regeneration time. The only way to reduce their cooldown is through this skill.
  • The Divine Gift starts at level 1. Whenever the character gains a level, the base level of the Divine Gift is set to the character's level.
    • The Character Editor does not update the base level of the Divine Gift when changing the character's level.
