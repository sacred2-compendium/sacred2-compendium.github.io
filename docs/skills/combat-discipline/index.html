Combat Discipline

Combat Discipline

Up to three combat arts can be combined and the combat arts in combos regenerate faster. Slightly increases the damage of all combat arts.

  • Regeneration Time -10.0%
  • Damage +X

At Level 75: Combat Discipline Mastery

Up to four combat arts can be combined. Enhances the effectiveness of all active effects.

Combat Discipline Mastery

Up to four combat arts can be combined and the combat arts in combos regenerate even faster. Increases the damage of all combat arts.

  • Regeneration Time -20.0%
  • Damage +X

Skill bonuses

Maximum Combo Size X

  • Without Combat Discipline, a maximum of 2 combat arts can be linked in a combo.
  • Learning the skill increases this limit to 3.
  • Mastering the skill raises the limit to the maximum of 4.

Regeneration Time -X%

  • Affects only combat arts that are placed in a combo. However, a combo can consist of a single combat art, which allows players to benefit from this bonus even if they don’t wish to use multi-art combos. There is no mechanical disadvantage to doing so.
  • This bonus is fixed at -10% before mastery and -20% after mastery. It does not scale with skill level.
  • The percentage is applied directly as a multiplier to combat art regeneration time.

Damage +X

  • Affects all combat arts, including buffs.
  • Combat arts do not need to be placed in a combo to benefit from this bonus.
  • The tooltip incorrectly suggests a flat damage bonus, but this is actually a percentage-based bonus. The displayed value is ten times the actual percentage. For example, a value of +1356 at level 75 corresponds to +135.6% damage.
  • Stacks additively with other percentage-based damage bonuses.

Additional notes

  • While Ancient Magic, Combat Discipline, and Concentration are classified as "combat art skills" in the game files and UI, the bonus Combat Art Skills +X does not increase their skill levels.
