Sinister Predator (Buff)

While active, Sinister Predator increases ranged attack speed and chance to hit with ranged weapons. It also adds a chance that opponents will drop shrunken heads.

Modification details

Eagle Eye

Increases the chance of critical hits.

Rapid Fire

Increases attack speed of ranged attacks.

Marked Shot

Adds a chance that opponents cannot evade or parry incoming shots.

Perforate

Chance that projectiles will pierce through their targets and continue on their flight path.

Hunting Focus

Decreases the regeneration time of the aspect Capricious Hunter.

Unflinching

Reduces the opponent's chance to deflect projectiles.
